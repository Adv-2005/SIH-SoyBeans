Electronic devices can not and should not be placed in the trash can with your normal waste. Along with the damage that can be caused to our environment, you could possibly be fined for doing this. Many recycling centers will accept electronic devices such as computers, printers, laptops, stereos, small appliances, and pretty much anything with a circuit board. You can search on this site to find a local facility that will accept your e-waste. An added benefit that many people are not aware of is that some facilities also will pay you cash for your old electronics because the circuit boards have small amounts of precious metals contained in them.
